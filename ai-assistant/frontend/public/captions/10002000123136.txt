 The image is about a medical condition called PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome). It is a hormonal disorder that affects women of childbearing age. The exact cause of PCOS is unknown, but it is thought to be caused by a combination of genetic and environmental factors.

The symptoms of PCOS can vary from woman to woman. Some of the most common symptoms include:

* Irregular periods
* Heavy bleeding
* Acne
* Hirsutism (excessive hair growth)
* Weight gain
* Difficulty losing weight
* Infertility

PCOS can be diagnosed with a blood test and a pelvic exam. Treatment for PCOS typically includes lifestyle changes, such as diet and exercise, and medication.
