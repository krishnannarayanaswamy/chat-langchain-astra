 The image is a table that summarizes the management of type 2 diabetes mellitus. It includes information on healthy eating, regular exercise, weight loss, diabetes medications and insulin therapy, blood sugar monitoring, and clinical monitoring.

For healthy eating, a balanced diet should be followed. Fibre-rich food should be incorporated more. Fewer refined grains and fewer calories should be consumed.

Regular exercise is essential to maintain a healthy weight. Children should have 60 minutes of moderate to vigorous aerobic exercise daily. This helps in better control of blood sugar levels, cholesterol, and triglycerides.

Weight loss is also important in the management of type 2 diabetes mellitus.

There are a number of different diabetes medications and insulin therapy options available. The choice of medication will depend on the individual's needs and preferences.

Blood sugar monitoring is essential in the management of type 2 diabetes mellitus. This can be done at home using a blood glucose meter.

Clinical monitoring is also important in the management of type 2 diabetes mellitus. This includes regular eye exams and podiatric evaluations.
