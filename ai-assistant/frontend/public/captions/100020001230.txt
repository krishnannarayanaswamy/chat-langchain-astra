 The introduction of the book "PrepLadder NEET PG 20th Edition: Clinical Quick Review" is written by Dr. Deepanshu Goyal, who is a part of Team PrepLadder. The book is designed to help medical students prepare for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test Postgraduate (NEET PG) examination. The introduction explains that the NEET PG pattern has been shifting from factual-based questions to more clinical-oriented questions, and that the book aims to provide students with a thorough understanding of the clinical approach to diseases. The book covers various topics, including physiology, pathology, medical and surgical treatments, and provides a quick summary of each topic. The introduction concludes by wishing the students the best for their NEET PG preparation.
