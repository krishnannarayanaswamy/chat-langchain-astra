 The image is about Addison's disease. It is a disorder of the adrenal glands. The adrenal glands are located on top of the kidneys. They produce hormones that help the body function properly. Addison's disease occurs when the adrenal glands do not produce enough of these hormones.

The symptoms of Addison's disease can include:
* Weight loss
* Fatigue
* Nausea
* Vomiting
* Diarrhea
* Abdominal pain
* Muscle weakness
* Joint pain
* Skin discoloration
* Depression
* Anxiety

Addison's disease is diagnosed with a blood test. Treatment for Addison's disease includes taking medication to replace the hormones that the adrenal glands are not producing.
