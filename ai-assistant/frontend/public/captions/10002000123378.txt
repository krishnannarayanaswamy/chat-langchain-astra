According to the image, the most common type of complicated cataract is posterior secondary cataract (PSC). The most common cause of PSC is chronic anterior uveitis. Other causes include high myopia, retinitis pigmentosa, Leber's amaurosis, gyrate atrophy, stickler syndrome, and toxic cataract. 
Toxic cataract can be caused by steroids, phenothiazines, long-acting miotics such as echothiophate, phospholine iodide, DFP, amiodarone, busulfan, gold, and chloroquine.
