 **Title:** Porphyria Cutanea Tarda
    
**Pattern of inheritance:** 
Autosomal dominant

**Enzyme Deficiency:** 
Uroporphyrinogen decarboxylase (UROD)

**Substrate accumulated:** 
Uroporphyrin

**Triggers:** 
- Alcohol
- Estrogens
- Hepatitis C
- HIV
- Sunlight exposure

**Clinical features:** 
- Photosensitivity
- Skin fragility,
- Blisters and erosions on sun-exposed areas
- Hyperpigmentation
- Tea-coloured urine

**Diagnosis:** 
- Urine test for elevated levels of uroporphyrin
- Genetic testing for UROD gene mutations

**Treatment:** 
- Phlebotomy for iron reduction
- Hydroxychloroquine
- Avoiding triggers
- Sun protection
- Treating any underlying conditions (e.g., hepatitis C, HIV)
